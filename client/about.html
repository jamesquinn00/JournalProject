Our Goal

Our goal is to build our journaling community to 10,000 writers within 2 years. We plan to expand into 7 continents and implement a live translation feature which allows users to read any post in their native tongue. We believe that there should be no barriers to communication, not even language!

Frequently Asked Queries

How do I post a blog?
Great question! We've made it very simple for you: just go to the homepage, enter your blog title and blog content in the indicated fields, then select a gif of your choice by typing your gif topic into the indicated box and click preview. Once you are happy with your gif, click submit and your blog will be live!
Can I add my name to the blog?
No, that's not possible unfortunately. Whilst we're sure your writing is excellent, our goal for the community is to create a safe, anonymous space where anybody can post anythere with complete freedom and without fear of judgement.
